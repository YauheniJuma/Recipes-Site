Shkmeruli

Shkmeruli is an old Georgian dish that is one of the most popular and sought-after dishes in Georgian restaurants. It should be noted that the name Shkmeruli comes from the village of Shkmeri in Racha.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

40min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Meat

instructions

step 1

To prepare Shkmeruli, clean the chicken well, cut it in half, and sprinkle salt on both sides. Place the chicken skin in a buttered pan over high heat to brown the skin quickly.

step 2

Put a unique, heavy cast-iron lid press on top; its diameter should be slightly smaller than the pan so that it touches the chicken directly.

step 3

If we don’t have such a lid put a heavy object on top. After 6-7 minutes, check on the chicken; if the skin is browned and crispy, flip it over, put the lid on the pot again and lower the heat.

step 4

Remove the fried chicken from the pan, cut it, and move it to a dish. In the pan where the chicken was fried, fry the crushed garlic in the remaining oil. The garlic should be juicy, so do not fry it a lot.

step 5

When the color changes a little, pour 1 cup of milk (for those who don’t like milk, water or 10% cream is good), let it boil for 2 minutes and dip the chicken into the sauce.

step 6

Mix the meat with all the mentioned ingredients and knead. Take 1 and 1/2 tablespoons of meat, place it in the middle of the dough, and wrap and fold it around the dough in accordion style.

step 7

Secure the top to prevent the contents from spilling out. Place the meatballs in a pot of boiling salted water. When they float to the surface (bottom side up) after about 7 minutes, they are done cooking.

ingredients

1 kilogram chicken

1 tablespoon crushed garlic

1 cup (250 ml) milk

Salt as needed

tools

Large Bowl

Pot

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups

Pan
